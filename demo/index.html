Pharmaceutical Products

TCS supplies a large range of products to the pharmaceutical sector.

Our sterile filtered serum is widely used in cell culture, microbiology procedures and diagnostic kits. Blood from our donor animals continues to play an important role as a nutritional supplement in microbiological culture media.

Human Serum and Plasma

TCS Biosciences Ltd offers a comprehensive range of Human Serum and Plasma to customers in the United Kingdom and Europe.

Human serum and plasma products have proved over many years to be suitable material for use in biochemical and in vitro diagnostic applications including EIA, IFA, CF, RIA and latex agglutination. They are also suitable for use as controls, standards and calibrators.

We offer a range from different countries of origin, to suit all types of budget from R&D to full FDA approved.
